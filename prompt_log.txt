**Mill Road Mill** is a [windmill](/w/Windmill "Windmill")
 north of [East Ardougne](/w/East_Ardougne "East Ardougne")
. It is run by [Milton the Miller](/w/Milton_the_Miller "Milton the Miller")
, a member of the radical [Humans Against Monsters](/w/H.A.M. "H.A.M.")
 group. [Richard](/w/Richard_(Ardougne) "Richard (Ardougne)")
 can be found in the mill selling basic farming supplies in his [shop](/w/Richard%27s_Farming_Shop "Richard's Farming Shop")
. There is a [statue of a king](/w/Statue_of_a_king_(generic) "Statue of a king (generic)")
 to the west.

Since the opening of the [Dorgesh-Kaan - Keldagrim Train System](/w/Dorgesh-Kaan_-_Keldagrim_Train_System "Dorgesh-Kaan - Keldagrim Train System")
, [Sigmund](/w/Sigmund "Sigmund")
 took up residence in a secret basement under the mill, with glyphs on the wall preventing teleportation. When [Grubfoot](/w/Grubfoot "Grubfoot")
 visited the [Plain of Mud](/w/Plain_of_Mud "Plain of Mud")
, he was abducted by the H.A.M. agents and taken to the base. At roughly the same time, [H.A.M. agent Walton](/w/H.A.M._agent_Walton "H.A.M. agent Walton")
 was sent to [Dorgesh-Kaan](/w/Dorgesh-Kaan "Dorgesh-Kaan")
, disguised as a [merchant](/w/Merchant_Walton "Merchant Walton")
 and started poisoning the children of the city, until he was arrested and even killed by [Zanik](/w/Zanik "Zanik")
, who had been influenced by [Bandos](/w/Bandos "Bandos")
 himself.

Later, an adventurer infiltrates the base to free Grubfoot, but, just as they leave, a fully possessed Zanik, the [Goblin High Priest](/w/High_Priest_(Bighead) "High Priest (Bighead)")
 and a small group of goblins break into the base and the [Assault on Sigmund's Base](/w/Assault_on_Sigmund%27s_Base "Assault on Sigmund's Base")
 begins, wherein all goblins and H.A.M. agents are killed, including Sigmund, and the High Priest becomes the [Bandos Avatar](/w/Bandos_Avatar "Bandos Avatar")
 after Zanik defied him. The basement is destroyed in an ensuing earthquake and can no longer be accessed afterwards.
